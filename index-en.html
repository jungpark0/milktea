I love milk tea 
Winter 2021 — first time into milk tea.
Tried many cafes since.
Prefer sweet, milky ones.
Started keeping record in Jan 2025.
10 cups, 10 cafes so far.
Wasn’t planning to start with milk tea. It just came up while thinking about what to put on the website. I happened to be drinking milk tea a lot at the time. Still keeping notes — places, flavors, stories, atmosphere.
The best kind of milk tea, for me, is royal milk tea. It’s a bit sweet, and overall just really smooth. The strong milk flavor tones down the typical tea scent. I never really liked the smell of black tea or Earl Grey in milk tea. But lately, I don’t mind it. The more I drink it, the more I get used to it. Feels like my taste is slowly changing.
I have my own standard for what is a good milk tea. It’s the royal milk tea from a place called Gabie. Gabie is a café near Hongdae. I used to spend a lot of time around there, so I’d go to the café pretty often. People usually order coffee at cafés, but I don’t like coffee, so I went with milk tea instead. That’s how I ended up trying Gabie’s royal milk tea. I ordered it without much thought, but it turned out to be really good. That was the first time I felt like milk tea could actually taste great.
